## What GRNDWRK Is

AI-powered operational performance dashboard for hospitality venues. Connects POS, workforce management, and reservations tools to deliver real-time P&L visibility, live labour tracking, reservations intelligence, and AI insight reports. Does not replace existing tools — connects and interprets them.

**Target market:** Independent and family-owned hospitality venues in Australia, $1M–$10M annual revenue. Owner-operators making financial decisions themselves.
